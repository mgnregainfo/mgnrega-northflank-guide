About MGNREGA Scheme

The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA) is one of the world's largest social security initiatives, designed to guarantee hundred days of manual employment in every financial year to adult members of any rural household who are willing to perform unskilled manual labor.
